When such a sum has a supremum over all \(\Sigma\)-partitions of \(X\text{,}\) we can define the integral of f with respect to \(\mu\) by
\begin{equation*} \int f \, d\mu = \sup \{\sum_{i=1}^m \mu(A_i) \inf_{A_j} f: A_1, \ldots A_m \text{ is a } \Sigma-\text{partition of } X\}. \end{equation*}
