Suppose that \(\gamma(t) = z_0 + R e^{it}\) parametrizes \(\gamma\text{.}\) By the integral theorem for derivatives, we have
\begin{align*} \abs{f^{n}(z_0)} \amp= | \frac{n!}{2\pi i} \oint_C \frac{f(z)}{(z - z_0)^{n+1}} \, dz |\\ \amp= \frac{n!}{2\pi} |\oint_C \frac{f(z)}{(z - z_0)^{n+1}} \, dz|\\ \amp\leq \frac{n!}{2\pi} \frac{M}{R^{n+1}} 2 \pi R,\\ \amp= \frac{n!}{R^n}M \end{align*}
where the inequality follows from the ML-inequality.
