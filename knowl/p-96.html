Boundary value problems associated with harmonic functions come with two varieties of standard conditions.

  1. Dirichlet problem: we specify the values of \(u\) at the boundary of \(A\text{.}\)

  2. Neumann problem: we specify the derviative of \(u\) at the boundary of \(A\text{,}\) in the sense that we specify

    \begin{equation*} \frac{\partial u}{\partial n} = \operatorname{grad} u \cdot n. \end{equation*}
