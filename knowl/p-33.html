Suppose that \(A, B\) are bounded, simply connected regions with boundaries \(\partial A\) and \(\partial B\) that are simple, continuous, closed curves. Then any conformal map of \(A\) that is one-to-one onto \(B\) can be extended to a continuous map of \(A \cup \partial A\) one-to-one onto \(B \cup \partial B\text{.}\)
