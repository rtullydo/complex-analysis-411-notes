Theorem 3.2.9. \(M\)-test, product form.

Let \(X\) be a set, and for all \(n\) let \(f_n: X \to \C\) be a bounded function. If for some finite \(M\) we have
\begin{equation*} \sum_{n=1}^\infty \sup_{x \in X} \abs{f_n(x) -1} \leq M \end{equation*}
then the partial products \(\prod_{n-1}^N f_n\) converge uniformly to \(\prod_{n=1}^\infty f_n\) on \(X\text{.}\)
