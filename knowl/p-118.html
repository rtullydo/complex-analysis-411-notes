One approach to constructing a solution on the disk is to precompose \(\Phi\) with the conformal map taking the disk to the upper half plane; that is, let \(f\) be the complex function on the unit disk given by
\begin{equation*} f = \Phi \circ C = 1 + \frac{ - 1}{\pi i} \log(i \frac{1+z}{1-z}+1) + \frac{1}{\pi i} \log(i \frac{1+z}{1-z} -1). \end{equation*}
