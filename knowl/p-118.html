Determine the image of the map \(z \mapsto \sin z\) on the half-strip with boundary \(0 \leq x \leq \pi/2, y = 0, y = \pi/2\) (you should get a quarter-plane: make sure to track where the boundary pieces map). Use this conformal map to find a harmonic function giving the temperature on the first quadrant, with boundary conditions \(T = 1\) on the positive imaginary axis, \(T\) is insulated on the interval \(0 \leq x \leq 1\text{,}\) and \(T = 0\) on the rest of the real axis. (Bonus, use a visualizer to draw the flow lines by finding the level curves of the harmonic conjugate of \(T\text{.}\))
