Proof

Because \(f\) is bijective, \(f\inv\) exists. Because \(f\) is conformal, for any \(z \in A\text{,}\) we must have \(f\dd(z) \neq 0\text{.}\) Then the inverse function theorem gives that \(f\inv\) is analytic and

\begin{equation*} \frac{d}{dw} f\inv(w) = \frac{1}{f\dd(z)} \neq 0, \end{equation*}

and so \(f\inv\) is also conformal.
