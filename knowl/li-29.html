Item 2.
For any \(k \geq 1\text{,}\) the series of \(k\)th derivatives \(\sum_n f_n^{(k)}\) converges on \(D\) and converges uniformly and absolutely on every closed and bounded subset of \(D\) to the function \(f^{(k)}\text{.}\) That is, the derivative of the limit is the limit of the derivatives.
